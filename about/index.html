About

Hey there! I’m Chetan and my story is of an immigrant who got a chance to live and work in three different countries around the world and finally decided to settle down in Toronto, Canada.

I work as a Cloud consultant and specialize in multi cloud architecture and automation. I help organizations and people embrace their cloud journey.

For the past 12 years I have been working with organizations of different shapes and sizes ranging from an early stage startup to a well established Enterprise operating in different domains - fintech, security, automaker, telecom, banking.

My humble beginnings were of a Unix administrator who started his career with an Enterprise organization right after graduating in Computer Science.

In 2016 I decided to move to Australia and landed my first professional job abroad with Bureau of Meteorology as a Senior Linux Engineer. It was quite a fulfilling role as the team I was working for was responsible for managing BOM’s infrastructure and making sure the Australian public has round the clock access to the weather data. I was working with different distros of Linux - Fedora, Ubuntu, RedHat to name some and it’s when I started working with Amazon Web Services and automation (Terraform, Ansible)

Nature has its own plans and it was 2019 when we decided to migrate again this time to Canada. We had a hard time deciding which coast to settle in - East or West and finally we made Toronto our home.

My experience here in North America has primarily been helping organizations build their cloud infrastructure following industry best practices and standards while also having a keen eye on security.

I provide consulting on various facets of cloud engineering for managed offerings from both Google Cloud Platform and Amazon Web Services. I help organizations architect their cloud infrastructure, automate the build process, build SRE/DevOps practices and help accelerate their cloud journey.

  • Orchestration: Kubernetes - Google Kubernetes Engine, Amazon Elastic Kubernetes Service, Nomad
  • Multi-cloud serverless: Google Cloud Run For Anthos, Cloud Run, AWS Lambda, AWS Fargate
  • Service Mesh: Google Anthos Service Mesh, Istio, Consul
  • Virtualization: Docker
  • Version Control: Github, GitLab
  • CI/CD: Google Cloud Build, Concourse, GitLab CI, AWS CodebBuild
  • Observability: Grafana, Prometheus, Google Cloud Monitoring, Stackdriver, AWS CloudWatch, Datadog
  • Package Management: Helm
  • Infrastructure as Code: Terraform
  • DNS: Cloud DNS, Route53
  • Cert Manager/SSL Management: Google Certificate Authority Service, AWS Certificate Manager
  • FaaS: Google Cloud Functions, AWS Lambda
  • Compute: Google Compute Engine, Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud (EC2)
  • IAM: Google Identity and Access Management, Amazon Identity and Access Management - AWS Organizations & AWS SSO
  • Secret management: Google Secret Manager, AWS Secrets Manager
  • Security & identity: Google Identity-Aware Proxy (IAP), AWS Systems Manager
  • Database: Google Cloud Spanner, Google Cloud SQL, Amazon RDS
  • In-memory data store: Google Memorystore, Amazon Elasticache

Beyond work I am a husband and a proud father who loves spending free time with his family. We all love going out for hikes and bicycle rides. I also play Cricket sometimes but have to admit I am not very good at it.
